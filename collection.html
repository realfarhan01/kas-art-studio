TRADITIONAL

Traditional design with universally recognized timeless beauty it in, no wonder that these rugs known for the luxurious touch of elegance and royal look they add to any room. Traditional rugs usually refer to either oriental, persian, european, victorian or french patterns.

    TRANSITIONAL

    Transitional rugs are more casual style that fills between traditional and contemporary. They are often adapted from traditional designs with a modern twist or reworking of traditional colors. The result is an enduring designs that is modern and classic blend striking the perfect balance.

      CONTEMPORARY

      Contemporary rugs are described as modern luxury and the designs consist with art work in tune with current modern aesthetics sensibilities and also the design can usually integrate bold color choices and unusual patterns it can fit seamlessly with furnishing and decor.
