Rugs & Carpets

The KAS name is a recent addition to the Luxury rug scene and its innovative profile is built on generations of expertise, KAS is a renowned rug manufacturer and has been in the industry for over 50 years. A relatively new name in contemporary carpet area is KAS ART STUDIO. and Recognized for their individual style and unique coloration.

KAS

KAS is an ode to the evolution of rug making over the decades that KAS family has witnessed the firm represents a rug making legacy that span four generations. An Commitment to a modern aesthetic creating statement and timeless collections.

KAS is obsessed with design details & artwork, driven to push the boundaries of color, palette & texture and form. Our creative art draw the attention from all corners of life that will enhance the spaces they are placed in, present and for the life time.
